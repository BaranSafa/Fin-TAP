// FAQ
What is a token?
A token is Fin-TAP's internal credit unit. Each AI prediction request consumes exactly 1 token, regardless of model type or asset class (stocks or crypto).
Do I need a credit card to sign up?
No. You receive 5 free tokens on registration with no payment required, giving you 5 immediate predictions to explore the platform.
Are cryptocurrency predictions available?
Yes! Fin-TAP now supports BTC, ETH, BNB, SOL, ADA, XRP, DOGE and AVAX — the same ML pipeline that powers stock predictions also works for crypto.
How accurate are the predictions?
Models are trained in real-time on 8+ years of historical data and validated on held-out test data. XGBoost and Gradient Boost typically achieve ~1% MAPE on backtesting. Always treat predictions as one signal, not financial advice.
Is the data always up to date?
Yes. Fin-TAP uses a smart cache: data refreshes every hour during market hours, and every 6 hours when markets are closed. No manual retraining needed — every prediction uses the latest available data.
